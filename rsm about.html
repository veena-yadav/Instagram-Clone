About Restaurant History

Restaurants are an institution in nearly every country and every culture in the world. The restaurant as we know it today, a place where people come to eat and drink and socialize, is credited to the French Revolution.But even before Marie Antoinette and Louis XVI were sent to the guillotine, restaurants had been around in one form or another for thousands of years. Since the 20th century, restaurants have undergone significant changes, in part to technological advancements in food productions. Today, there is a movement in many restaurants for a return to the farms, placing greater emphasis on local goods.
